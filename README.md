# Philosophy

Users are inherently driven by a desire to be able to communicate in another language. (Epic Meaning)


Secondary motivators include


* Social - the joy of making something useful and sharing it with a friend; competitive spirit
  - Shareable UGC
  - Leaderboards
* Accomplishment - the joy of aquiring and mastering new skills
  - Vocab mastery tracking
  - Grammar mastery tracking
  - Badges & trophies
* Empowerment - the joy of learning what you want and applying it in ways that are unique to your personality
  - Open-ended quests, reactive and deep NPCs
  - Easy UGC creation for specific topics
  - Inline AI tutoring while trying to speak the language and making mistakes
